# PRD — AI Ops Site for Small Agencies

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** March 2026
**Status:** Draft

---

## 1. Product Overview

### Vision

A content-driven resource site helping small agencies and service businesses implement practical AI systems for operations, client delivery, and internal workflows. No hype — only actionable systems, tools, and templates.

### Target Audience

- Small agencies (2–10 people)
- Boutique studios and service businesses
- Operators and founders looking to systematize with AI
- Teams that want implementation, not theory

### Core Value Proposition

Practical, no-fluff AI operations content designed for small teams, focused on implementation over inspiration.

## 7. Technical Requirements

### Functional Requirements

- Responsive design (mobile-first)
- Newsletter signup integration (email pro)
- Lead magnet delivery (gated download)
- Template library with category filters
- Blog/article system with category taxonomy
- SEO-optimized page structure (meta titles, descriptions, OG tags)
- Analytics integration

### Non-Functional Requirements

- Page load time under 3 seconds
- Clean, minimal design — professional, not flashy
- Accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- SSL/HTTPS

### Future Technical Needs (post-launch)

- Affiliate link management
- Payment processing for paid templates
- Service booking/waitlist system
- Search functionality across content
